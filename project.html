What am I Doing?

Since August 2020, I have been investing a small sum of money in stocks. The goal of this has never been to get rich overnight but rather to attempt to build a track record proving my different ideas and theories. Over the years, my methods of evaluating companies has grown more and more complicated. Still, the essence of my idea has remained the same: Long-term investments in companies with high market dominance in markets I believe will grow crucial in the next vicennial or so. While doing this, I am unfortunately/fortunately doomed to pick companies in industries with booms. These booms have forced me to devest in a few companies/markets I would have rather stayed in if the valuations had stayed reasonable(but that's of cause a luxury problem to have).

End Goal

The end goal of this project is to build a track record so solid that I will be able to attract investors to a future investment fund, which I intend to start.
